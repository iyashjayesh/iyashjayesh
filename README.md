### ✨&nbsp; About Me

Hi, I’m Yash 👋

I see myself as a professional student. I learn deeply, think independently, and teach what I understand. I often surprise myself with how well-formed my ideas are, and I know they will continue to evolve as I grow, reflect, and spend more time building on them.

I’m a backend developer with 4+ years of experience building enterprise applications and contributing to open-source software.

I started my career in 2022 with Golang, a language I hadn’t even heard of before. That moment changed how I approached programming. Instead of chasing frameworks, I focused on fundamentals, system design, and first-principles thinking. That foundation still shapes how I build software today.

I’m broadly interested in technology, SaaS, psychology, and learning how to make perfect pancakes. I enjoy exploring new tools, building small but meaningful projects, and working independently. Most of my work is solo by choice.

My preferred tech stack includes Golang, Next.js, and MongoDB. Outside of work, I enjoy traveling, cooking, and experimenting with ideas. I hold a degree in Computer Science and Engineering, have interned at tech companies in Bangalore, India, and have participated in 27+ hackathons purely for the challenge and fun.

#### Opensource

All of my projects are released as open-source on GitHub:

- [go-adaptive-pool](https://github.com/iyashjayesh/go-adaptive-pool) - **go-adaptive-pool** A production-grade adaptive worker pool for Go that handles dynamic scaling, backpressure, metrics and safe shutdown under load. It's built to keep your system stable when traffic spikes by not letting goroutines grow out of control.

- [MoniGo](https://github.com/iyashjayesh/monigo) - **MoniGo** is a performance monitoring library for Go apps, offering real-time insights into service-level and function-level metrics. An intuitive UI enables developers to track and optimize performance. Get your Go app's dashboard up in just 10 seconds! 

- [gocron-ui](https://github.com/go-co-op/gocron-ui) - **gocron-ui** is a lightweight, real-time web interface for monitoring and controlling gocron scheduled jobs. It provides a complete solution for visualizing job schedules, tracking execution status, and managing jobs through an intuitive web dashboard.
